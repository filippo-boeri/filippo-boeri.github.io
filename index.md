<div class="home-bio" markdown="1">

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics at [City St George's, University of London](https://www.citystgeorges.ac.uk/about/schools/policy-global-affairs/economics) and a Visiting Fellow in the Department of Geography and Environment at the [LSE](https://www.lse.ac.uk/).

My main research interests lie at the intersection of urban economics, economic geography and international trade, with a particular emphasis on how technological shocks drive rising inequalities across regions and different socio-economic groups.

My current research focuses on the following areas:
- The relationship between automation and reshoring dynamics
- How remote work affects productivity and local labor markets
- The evolution of co-agglomeration forces in nineteenth-century Britain
- How population aging shapes technology adoption and youth migration
